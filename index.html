Connect Four

Rules

  • 2 players (blue and red) alternate turns.
  • On each turn, a player drops a game piece corresponding to their player color in the top of a selected column.
  • This piece will fall down to the furthest-down unoccupied cell.

Game End

  • The game is won when a player makes four in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
  • The game ends in a tie if the entire board is filled up without a winner.

Instructions

To place a piece in a column, click the topmost cell of that column.
